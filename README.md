## 🧠 About Me

I'm an **undergraduate researcher** and **data scientist** passionate about making AI trustworthy, interpretable, and clinically useful. I spend my days working at the intersection of **AI alignment**, **large language models**, and **biomedical informatics**.

- 🏛️ **Undergraduate Researcher** — Harvard Medical School & Massachusetts General Hospital  
  *Chain-of-Thought robustness in LLMs for clinical applications*
- 🤖 **Member** — MIT AI Alignment  
- 🎓 **B.S. Data Science** — UC San Diego, Halıcıoğlu Data Science Institute (Ranked #8, U.S. News) · 2024 – 2028  
  *VP of Research, AI Student Collective · ACM · IEEE · UBIC · SDxUCSD*
- 🏥 **Former Research Intern** — Dartmouth Health & Dartmouth Cancer Center  
  *RAG for Pathology Reports*
- 🌐 Founder of **[Labry](https://www.linkedin.com/company/109509095)** — democratizing scientific research
